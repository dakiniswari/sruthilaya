About Us

Music is divinity for us

Surithilaya Music School was founded by Mr. Muthukumaran and Mrs. Mahalakshmi Muthukumaran. This music academy is keenly teaching classical music to all age groups since 2000. Our guru, Mrs. Mahalakshmi Muthukumaran has done Graduation in Music and is serving classical music for more than 30+ years. She teaches vocal and violin for more than 100+ students. Students have cleared many grade exams under her guidance.

"Where words leave off, music begins."
